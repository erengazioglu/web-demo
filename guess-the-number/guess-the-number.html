I pick a number, you guess it.

It's that simple. I'll pick a number in a certain range, you will try to guess that number. You can try a number of times before you run out of guesses, after which it is game over.

The rules are as follows:

  • I pick a number ranging from 1 to 100 (inclusive),
  • You have 10 chances to guess correctly.
  • After every incorrect guess, I'll tell you whether you went too high or too low.

When you're ready, type in your name and start the game!
